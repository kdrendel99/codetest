Picking a coding language

Picking a coding language to begin with may seem like a daunting task. With so many options available it's difficult to denote where to begin. New languages are being tweaked, reinvented, and published frequently and it's important to be able to stay up-to-date in order to survive as a web developer. Being able to write in different languages is important, but what if you don't know where to start?
